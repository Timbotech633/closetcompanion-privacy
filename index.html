🌟 App Overview

ClosetCompanion is a personal wardrobe management application designed to help you organize, catalog, and manage your clothing collection. Our app operates with a privacy-first approach, ensuring all your personal data remains secure and under your complete control.

Developer: TimboTech
App Package: com.timbotech.closetcompanion
Platform: Android (Google Play Store)

📷 Camera Permission Usage

Our app requests camera permission (android.permission.CAMERA) for the following specific purposes:

  • 📸Clothing Photography: Capture photos of your clothing items to add to your personal wardrobe collection
  • 👕Wardrobe Documentation: Create visual records of your garments for easy identification and organization
  • ✨Outfit Planning: Take photos to plan and save outfit combinations
  • 🏷️Item Categorization: Visual documentation to help categorize and sort your clothing items

🔒 Data Storage & Privacy Commitment

Local Storage Only

  • 📱Device-Only Storage: All photos, data, and personal information are stored exclusively on your device
  • 🚫No Cloud Upload: Images and wardrobe data are NEVER transmitted to external servers
  • 🔐Offline Operation: The core wardrobe management functionality works completely offline
  • 🏠Your Device, Your Data: You maintain complete ownership and control of all your information

Scoped Storage Implementation (Android API 35+)

  • Modern Security: Uses Android's latest scoped storage system for enhanced security
  • Limited Access: App can only access files it creates or files you explicitly share
  • Automatic Cleanup: App data is automatically removed when you uninstall the app

📊 Data Collection & Sharing

What We DON'T Collect

  • 📷Personal photos or wardrobe images
  • 📍Location data or GPS information
  • 👤Personal identification information
  • 💳Financial or payment information
  • 📞Contact lists or communication data
  • 🕐Usage patterns or behavioral analytics on personal data

Limited Data Usage

  • Advertising: We display ads through Google AdMob. No personal wardrobe data is shared with advertisers
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data may be collected to improve app stability (no personal content)
  • Performance: Basic app performance metrics (no personal data included)

🛡️ Security Measures

  • 🔐Android Scoped Storage: Utilizes Android's secure scoped storage system
  • 🏠Local Processing: All image processing and data management happens exclusively on your device
  • 🚫No Network Transmission: Personal photos and wardrobe data never leave your device
  • 🛡️Permission Management: Requests only essential permissions for core functionality
  • 🔒Data Encryption: Follows Android's built-in security and encryption standards

📱 Required Permissions

Permission Purpose Data Usage
CAMERA Take photos of clothing items Photos stored locally only
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES Access saved wardrobe photos Local file access only
INTERNET Display advertisements Ad content only, no personal data

👤 Your Rights & Control

  • 🗑️Delete Data: Remove any photos or wardrobe data at any time through the app
  • ⚙️Permission Control: Revoke camera or storage permissions in your device settings
  • 📤Data Export: Export or backup your wardrobe data locally
  • 🔄Data Portability: Your data remains in standard formats for easy migration
  • ❌App Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

🎯 Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements to support free app usage
  • Data Shared: Only anonymous device identifiers and ad interaction data
  • Personal Data: NO wardrobe photos or personal content is shared
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Our app is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.
